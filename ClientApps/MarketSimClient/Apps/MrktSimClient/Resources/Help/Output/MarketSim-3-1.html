Preface

The MarketSim Analyst Guide provides practical guidelines for integration of DecisionPower's MarketSim software with your Marketing strategies. This manual is designed to assist you in navigating MarketSim applications and applying the parameters necessary to run simulations for a variety of Marketing purposes.

This preface describes the MarketSim Analyst Guide in the following topics:

  • Using This Manual
  • Terminology
  • Contact Information
Using This Manual

Marketsim is a sophisticated yet intuitive, statistical analysis platform that enables you to mix and match the marketing components you use to forecast business results and marketing planning. This manual provides guidelines for using MarketSim's prospective scenario simulation tools to create your own marketing mix models, for presentations that best suit your requirements.

This user guide assists you with installation, navigation, and usage of the MarketSim software, in the following chapters:

Chapter
Purpose
Chapter 1-Installation and Setup

Describes requirements for your client computer and use guidelines to install MarketSim software.

Chapter 2-Getting Started

Describes how to work from the start of a project through simulation by using the MarketSim graphical user interface. Use this chapter as your navigation reference for locating tools for managing MarketSim projects, models, market plans, scenarios, simulations, and results.

Chapter 3-Editing Marketing Plans and Scenarios

Provides details about configuration for models, scenarios, and market plans.

Chapter 4-Working with Simulations

Describes the tools you use to create and modify simulations, and how to use your simulations.

Chapter 5-Viewing Simulation Results

Provides guidelines for configuring various methods for displaying results of your simulations.

Terminology

MarketSim uses terminology described in this section.

Term
Description
brand manager
Marketing personnel who receive direction from the category manager, and use BrandManager to create Trial Scenarios from which to generate forecast simulations.
category manager
Marketing personnel responsible for creating high-level, comprehensive market plans. These plans contain the simulated data generated from the Decision Power MarketSim program. Chunks of simulated data are assigned to brand manager(s) for use in creating Trial Scenarios.
category plan
The combination of existing product marketing plans-for your company as well as competitive companies-and forecasts is referred to as the operating Category Plan. This plan is the baseline used by BrandManager to form other market scenarios from which to generate simulated market projections.
market plan
Market Plans are created with the MarketSim program, to define numerous components pertinent to the business of advertising, promoting, and selling products. The combination of multiple market plans and forecasts forms the Category Plan.
Market Plans are typically categorized as Operating Marketing Plans and Expected Marketing Plans.
trial scenario
Trial Scenarios are created by the brand manager The results of Trial Scenarios are based on the application of scenarios created with BrandManager against market plan data provided from the category manager. The data generated from BrandManager scenarios can be output as .xls files or plot files.
